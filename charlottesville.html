WHAT IS CVILLIZATION?

Cvillization is an interactive online resource hub to explore Charlottesville’s growing innovation network. It utilizes an interactive map to find places in the greater Charlottesville and University of Virginia communities that foster innovative ideas and invigorate our community. Explore, find and connect with fellow innovators in the Charlottesville area to turn your next idea into reality, find that dream career, or simply learn more about the ground-breaking companies growing in Charlottesville.

WHY CHARLOTTESVILLE?

Charlottesville is ranked eighth as the city with the highest Venture Capital money deals. (Source:Richard Florida) Complementing the innovation already occurring within Charlottesville, the University of Virginia is committed to leveraging their existing resources from academic departments, faculty research, and student led initiatives to emerge as a leader in educating the next generation of social entrepreneurs and supporting the entrepreneurship ecosystem thriving in the region.
