WHAT IS CVILLIZATION?

Cvillization is an interactive online resource hub to explore Charlottesville’s growing innovation network. It utilizes an interactive map to find places in the greater Charlottesville and University of Virginia communities that foster innovative ideas and invigorate our community. Explore, find and connect with fellow innovators in the Charlottesville area to turn your next idea into reality, find that dream internship, or simply learn more about the cool ideas being generated in Charlottesville.

WHY CHARLOTTESVILLE?

With 20% of the state’s biotech companies, Charlottesville is ranked eighth as the city with the highest Venture Capital money deals. Complementing the innovation already occurring within Charlottesville, the University of Virginia is committed to leveraging their existing resources from academic departments, faculty research, and student led initiatives to emerge as a leader in educating the next generation of social entrepreneurs.
